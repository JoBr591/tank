Tag

E
359,818 players, 1,219,199 plays 19 playing now, 385 most ever online
303
Share
462 favourites
Joeriri Published on 28 Apr, 2015
❤Follow(201)

This is a local multiplayer game for 2 to 4 friends! One of you is the tagger and needs to chase the other players! When a player is tagged, they become the new tagger. Beware, when the timer runs out, the tagger loses!

Play tag in 9 different maps featuring a variety of jelly platforms which you can jump through, speed you up or make you jump high! Switch things up by randomizing the platforms, granting double-jumps or allowing collisions!

Full keyboard and controller support.

Instructions

Keyboard:

Menu

W A S D, T F G H, I J K L, Up Arrow Right Arrow Down Arrow Left Arrow: Join / Navigate

Enter: Confirm

Game

W A S D, T F G H, I J K L, Up Arrow Right Arrow Down Arrow Left Arrow: Move left or right / Jump / Fall through pink jelly

Esc / P: Pause

Controllers:

Menu

Start: Join

Left Stick: Navigate

A: Confirm

Game

Left Stick A X: Move left or right / Jump / Fall through pink jelly

Start: Pause
